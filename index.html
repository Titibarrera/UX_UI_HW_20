Beginner Classes

6-Week Beginner Wheel Throwing

We will begin with introductions and a basic wheel demonstration by one of our instructors. You will learn the basic steps of throwing a cylinder on the potter's wheel before trying it out yourself.

Intermediate Classes

6-Week Intermediate Wheel Throwing

Take your skills to the next level in this series of intermediate pottery classes. You'll get to take a deeper study into wheel throwing in this six-week course.

Design Process

Make your piece: In classes, you will refine your skills at the pottery wheel making functional pieces like bowls, plates, and mugs.

Glaze: In classes, you will learn glazing techniques to give your pottery color and make your ceramics waterproof.

Fire: In classes, you will fire your own clay pieces to a high temperature. The final aim is to heat the piece to the point that the clay and glazes are mature.
